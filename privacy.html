Introduction

This privacy policy applies to our video streaming website, which utilizes YouTube's API and videos for a collage project created by four team members.

Information Collected

We collect both personal and non-personal information from users. Personal information may include names, email addresses, etc. Non-personal information may include IP addresses, browser type, device information, etc. We also collect information through YouTube's API, such as video views and interactions.

Use of Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including user authentication, communication, analytics, site improvement, displaying videos, and tracking views.

Third-Party Services

Our website utilizes YouTube's API and videos. Please review YouTube's terms of service and privacy policy for information on how they handle user data.

Data Sharing

User data may be shared with YouTube as per their API terms. No other third-party sharing is conducted unless required by law or with user consent.

User Controls

Users have control over their data, including options to edit or delete account information and opt-out mechanisms for data collection/tracking.

Security Measures

We employ security measures such as encryption of data transmissions and secure storage practices to protect user information.

Updates to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of any changes.
